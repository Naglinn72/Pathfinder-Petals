About us at Pathfinder Pedals

Welcome to Pathfinder Pedals - Your Gateway to Mountain Biking Adventures! At Pathfinder Pedals, we're more than just a mountain biking company; we're a community of adventurers and thrill-seekers, united by our passion for exploring the great outdoors. Founded in 2015 in the heart of the Rocky Mountains, our mission has been to bring the excitement and beauty of mountain biking to everyone, from beginners to seasoned pros.
